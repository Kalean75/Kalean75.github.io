About me

"Warm it up, everything you've got. C'mon you apes, you wanna live forever?"


My name is Devin White, I was born and raised in Salt Lake City, Utah, as the middle child of 3 brothers.

I graduated highschool in 2012, and then attended the University of Utah, where I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Geoscience.

After teaching myself Python and messing around with developing game prototypes in Unity over the course of a summer, I found I had a passion for programming

I found teaching myself how to program fairly difficult, and decided I needed to take a more structured approach, As such, I decided to return to School to pursue a second Degree in Computer Science

Currently, I am working as a Metallurgist while going to school full time.

In my free time, I can usually be found skiing in the winter, or hiking, biking, camping, or floating white water in the spring and summer.

I also am an avid gamer, and play a lot when I'm not outside, accordingly, one of my dreams is to eventually develop an indie game using the skills I've learned in both school and on my own.
